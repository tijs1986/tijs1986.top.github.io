THE SKELETON CLIQUE FANBASE

The Skeleton Clique is the official name of the fanbase of Twenty One Pilots.
The Clique officially started during Regional at Best, with the introduction of Tyler
and Josh's signature skeleton hoodies. However, in the Blurryface Era,
the logo of the Clique was formed of a skeleton and alien.
As evidenced by the masks worn during performances, the skeleton represents Tyler,
while the alien represents Josh.
